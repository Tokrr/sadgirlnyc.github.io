ABOUT

I'm a self-taught front end developer who enjoys building user experiences with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I care deeply about learning and although I'm a late bloomer when it comes to coding, I reached my first goal in 2017 when I began my front-end career at Spotible, where I coded, designed, modified, and debugged hundreds of custom, cross-browser, responsive ads for highly trafficked websites including but not limited to NYT, The Atlantic, TMZ and National Geographic.

I am very proud of my progress and work but like any newb, you can always stand to learn more and faster! My newest goal is to become a Full Stack Developer in 2018! TBH, I am not comfortable with languages that go beyond the front-end ones and so I decided to start this 30 day coding challenge on github so that I can put to use everything I know so far while learning and applying new programming concepts, unfamiliar languages, trendy frameworks, cool APIs to build new and eventually more complicated projects that will reign in simplicity in the lives of others.

I'm happiest when I am creating and collaborating with diverse teams of developers, designers, musicians, film-makers, visionaries and product owners to build products and fulfill purposeful visions. My biggest fear up until 2 years ago was being creative. If you were to ask me now what my biggest fear is, I'd say not creating lol. How quickly things change!
